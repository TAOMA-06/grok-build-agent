# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project. Be respectful, assume good intent, discuss technical decisions with evidence, and never publish another person's private information or credentials.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and sexualized or insulting conduct. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's private contact channel. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and without retaliation.
